COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, process, and what makes ARCHSHADES unique.

What services does ARCHSHADES provide?

+

We offer comprehensive architecture and interior design services including residential design, commercial spaces, space planning, architectural design, turnkey solutions, and renovation projects. Our services cover everything from initial concept and 3D visualization to final execution and installation.

How long does a typical project take?

+

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A residential apartment typically takes 2-3 months from design to completion, while larger commercial projects may require 4-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during the initial consultation and maintain transparent communication throughout.

What is your design process?

+

Our process begins with an in-depth consultation to understand your vision, requirements, and budget. We then create detailed design concepts with 3D visualizations for your approval. Once finalized, we manage all aspects of execution including procurement, vendor coordination, and quality control to ensure flawless delivery.

Do you provide 3D visualizations?

+

Yes! We create photorealistic 3D renderings and visualizations of your space before any work begins. This allows you to see exactly how the finished project will look and make any desired changes to the design before implementation.

What areas do you serve?

+

While we're based in Sector 47, Noida, we serve clients throughout Delhi NCR including Noida, Greater Noida, Delhi, Ghaziabad, and Gurgaon. We also take on select projects in other major cities across India. Contact us to discuss your location.

How much do your services cost?

+

Pricing varies based on project scope, size, materials, and complexity. We offer customized packages to suit different budgets while maintaining our quality standards. Schedule a consultation for a detailed quote tailored to your specific requirements.
